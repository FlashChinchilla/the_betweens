09 // Knowledge Base

FAQ.

Is an internet connection required to play?
No, the core narrative experience is fully playable offline. An internet connection is only required for initial verification, cloud saves, and syncing your anomaly logs to the global leaderboards.
Will choices from the Beta carry over?
Due to the cascading nature of our choice architecture, Beta saves will not be compatible with the final 1.0 release structure. All timelines will be reset upon global deployment.
Is there multiplayer or co-op?
The Betweens is strictly a single-player narrative experience. However, an asynchronous global system will show you the percentage of other players who made the same timeline-altering decisions as you.
Will there be mod support?
Official mod tools are planned post-launch. We intend to give the community access to our node-based narrative engine to create custom Echo dimension scenarios.
Can I replay specific chapters to alter the timeline?
Yes, a "Quantum Rewind" feature unlocks upon your first full playthrough. This allows you to revisit specific pivot points without erasing your core timeline, enabling exploration of alternate consequences.
