01 // The Architecture

The Game.

Absolute consequence.
Zero illusion of safety.

The Betweens is a purely user-driven narrative experience where every decision fundamentally alters the fabric of the world.

A mysterious ring pulls a university student from Yangon into a parallel version of his own city. The narrative branching architecture calculates your morality, shaping endings based on how you interact with themes of life, survival, love, family, friends, and your partner.

Will you merge the worlds... or watch one disappear forever?
