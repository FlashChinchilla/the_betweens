Beyond Reality

Discover the lore, the souls bound to the ring, and the mind behind the parallel worlds of Yangon.

The Core Experience

Every heartbeat is a choice. Every choice is a consequence.

User Driven

The Betweens is not just a story you read; it is a reality you architect. From the moment the mysterious ring binds to you, the narrative fractures into countless possibilities based purely on your interactions.

There is no "correct" path. The game engines tracks your psychological profile through minor choices—how you speak to a stranger, whether you trust an old friend—and dynamically alters the environment of the Echo City.

Choices Matter

The butterfly effect is real. A seemingly insignificant decision in Chapter 1 can lead to the salvation or demise of a key character in the Final Chapter. The parallel Yangon is unforgiving.

Will you prioritize your Family and Friends, risking the fabric of your own universe? Will you pursue Love or a Partner in a world that might cease to exist? Or will you choose to Live your Life safely, letting the parallel dimension consume itself? The endings are vast, and entirely yours to bear.
