Directives

Operational Privacy Parameters. Absolute data sovereignty ensures your reality remains untampered.

01 //

Zero-Data Architecture

The Betweens is constructed on an absolute zero-data acquisition model. We do not harvest, monitor, aggregate, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII). Your navigational choices, timeline adjustments, and moral divergences are processed entirely within the local confines of your hardware node.

// No analytics telemetry
// No background profiling
// Total execution isolation
02 //

Hardware Perimeter

In order to maintain environmental resonance, the application executes requests strictly for audio output routing. At no point will the software request, access, or initialize microphone recording, camera capturing, or background location tracking.

Audio permissions are exclusively designated for the projection of the dynamic Echo dimension soundtrack and localized cinematic spatial audio.

// Audio Output: Authorized
// Audio Input (Mic): Strictly Prohibited
// Visual Input (Camera): Strictly Prohibited
03 //

Local Save States

All timeline progression, character affinity algorithms, and Quantum Rewind anchors are serialized and vaulted entirely on your local storage drive. Cloud synchronization protocols (if enabled by the user via third-party platform layers) utilize encrypted packets completely obfuscated from our developer network.

04 //

Communication Protocol

Any direct transmissions initiated by the user (via Waitlist submission or Support Comms) are handled transparently through the user's native email client. We retain email addresses exclusively for requested broadcast updates regarding game patches or Beta phase access. We do not distribute, sell, or cross-reference this communication data.

05 //

Directive Amendments

Should structural updates require modifications to this operational framework, all architectural changes will be systematically logged on this terminal prior to deployment. The integrity of your isolation remains our prime directive.
